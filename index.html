The Crisis

The Hidden Experts of Every Village

Every village holds invaluable wisdom that disappears when its custodians retire or pass away. India has banks for money, land, and documents — but no bank for local wisdom.

The Farmer's Secret

A farmer who discovered a technique to double crop yield. When he retires, decades of soil wisdom vanishes with him.

The SHG Leader

A woman who built a successful Self-Help Group business model. Her entrepreneurial insights are never documented.

The Transformative Teacher

A teacher who reduced school dropouts through innovative methods. His pedagogical breakthroughs are lost to time.

The Master Electrician

An electrician who trained dozens of youth in skilled trades. His training methodology dies with his retirement.

The Solution

A Knowledge Bank for Every Village

Create a digital and physical "Knowledge Bank" that captures, preserves, and distributes grassroots innovation across every level of governance.

Monthly Knowledge Capture

Every month, field teams visit villages to document and record invaluable local knowledge that would otherwise be lost forever.

  • Record success stories from local achievers
  • Document proven farming techniques
  • Capture local innovations and hacks
  • Preserve traditional water conservation
  • Archive village entrepreneurship models
